FocusDo Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FocusDo ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use the FocusDo iOS app.

1. Data We Process

FocusDo is designed to be privacy-first. Most data is stored on your device.

  • Task, habit, and focus data: titles, notes, schedule/reminder settings, completion history, and focus records that you create in the app.
  • App settings: onboarding state, reminder preferences, sync preference, and local feature settings.
  • Subscription status data: product ID, transaction ID, purchase/expiration dates, and entitlement status required to provide Pro access.
  • Local diagnostics counters: on-device event counters (for example, paywall views or restore attempts) used only to show in-app metrics.

2. In-App Purchases and Billing

Subscriptions and payments are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

We process subscription transaction metadata strictly to verify entitlement, restore purchases, and maintain subscription status in the app.

3. iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your app data may be synced via Apple CloudKit under your Apple ID. You can disable iCloud sync in the app settings at any time.

4. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, FocusDo schedules local reminders on your device (tasks, habits, and focus sessions). Notification permission can be changed in iOS Settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your app content with third-party advertisers or external analytics SDKs.

Data may be processed by Apple services required for app functionality, including StoreKit and CloudKit.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your local app data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • If iCloud sync is enabled, synced data retention is governed by your Apple account and Apple iCloud settings.
  • You can remove pending reminders from the in-app Account section.

7. Security

We use Apple platform security features and Keychain storage for sensitive subscription-related transaction records. No method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8. Children

FocusDo is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data in violation of applicable law, contact us so we can address the issue.

9. International Use

If you use iCloud sync or Apple billing services, data may be processed by Apple in accordance with Apple's own policies and infrastructure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect product, legal, or operational changes. The "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: support@focusdo.app

You can update this email to your official support address at any time.
